Welcome To Pancitan Ni Elmer

Little Story

Pancitan kay Elmer has been in the industry for almost three decades and they have been the go-to Panciteria ever since. Pancitan kay Elmer started when the owners, Elmer and Lolita started their own family. Elmer is solely the cook of the said Panciteria and this is the main reason why they are still in the industry and is a well-known Panciteria in Marikina. Since Elmer is fond of cooking they decided to start their own Pancitan a year after their first child was born. They are considered as a team since the future of their family depends on it. Luckily, Lolita has the experienced in handling and managing a business since she studied Hotel and Restaurant Management in Polytechnic University of the Philippines.
